# wiki (core knowledge model)

The central architecture of proactive-context: an ever-evolving wiki of many interlinked, topic-specific guides (modeled on nvk/llm-wiki), explicitly NOT a single synthesized PRODUCT_MODEL.md — the single-doc approach was rejected because it flattens nuance and dilutes everything into one generic file. In the v0.4 reframe, the wiki becomes a living, regenerable spec where every assertion is anchored to a verbatim passage of the conversation that justifies it.
