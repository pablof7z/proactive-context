# GuideFrontmatter

The YAML frontmatter of a wiki guide with exactly 11 fields: title, slug, summary, tags, volatility (hot|warm|cold), confidence (high|medium|low), created, updated, verified, compiled_from, sources. No see-also field exists in the struct; unknown keys are silently dropped.
