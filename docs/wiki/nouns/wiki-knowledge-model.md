# wiki (knowledge model)

An ever-evolving wiki of many interlinked, topic-specific deep guides (the llm-wiki model) replacing the single generic PRODUCT_MODEL.md. Capture grows it; inject navigates it: reads the index of guide titles, recognizes the relevant one(s), reads them, follows See-Also links. Knowledge is anchored and grown, not dumped and diluted.
