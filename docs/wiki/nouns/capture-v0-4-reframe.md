# capture (v0.4 reframe)

Reframed from 'extract lessons so the assistant stops repeating mistakes' into 'continuously reverse-engineer the complete, organized product specification from the conversation, losing no human-supplied nuance.' The governing principle is distill-don't-dump: capture refines the messy transcript stream into high-signal lessons before anything becomes retrievable, never raw-indexing conversation logs.
