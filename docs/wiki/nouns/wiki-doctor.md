# wiki doctor

A consolidation and maintenance tool that runs periodically and off the hot path; detects near-duplicate guide clusters via embedding cosine similarity, confirms merges via an LLM call, and merges confirmed clusters while preserving citations; operates in detect-only, dry-run, or --apply modes
